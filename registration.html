  • Please note that each accepted paper needs a full registration by or on Oct. 10, 2016. Participants’ registration fee includes:
    • Conference Proceedings
    • Welcome Reception
    • Coffee Breaks
    • Conference Banquet
    Please note Student Member or Student Non Member Registration does not include conference banquet.

    • 林守德 - 機器學習概論與推薦系統應用 - 11/25
    • 李宏毅 - 深入淺出深度學習 - 11/26
  • According to the Conference Registration Policy, each accepted TAAI 2016 paper must have at least one full registration of the TAAI/IEEE/JSAI Member or TAAI/IEEE/JSAI Non Member before or on Oct. 10, 2016 in order to be included in the conference proceedings (that means, discounted Student Registration does not apply in this case ).
    • Papers that do not have an associated registered author will not be included in the proceedings.
    • Registering late may mean that your paper may not appear in the proceedings. Please ensure that you complete your registration early.
    • One Full Registration by an associated author can cover up to two papers (that means, other additional papers need another full registration).
    • Students are required to provide a copy of a valid ID that certifies their full-time student status.
    • The maximum number of pages is eight (8). Up to two additional pages will be permitted for a charge of TWD 1,500 per additional page.
    • If you have any questions about registration,please contact us.
  • Payment type is "Credit Card" and after you complete your transaction, your confirmation will be sent to your e-mail.
  • Registration as a Student Non-Member will automatically become a TAAI Student Member.
  • Cancellations must be made by sending email through contact.
    • Cancellation will deduct 10 % handling fee from the refund.
    • No refunds will be given after November 14, 2016.
    • Registration fees covering the publication of one or more papers CANNOT be cancelled. All refunds will be handled after the conference.
